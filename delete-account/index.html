Delete Your Account

You can delete your Trinitarian account and associated data at any time using either of the options below.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. Your profile, saved sermons, downloads, comments and history will be removed and cannot be recovered.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

Open the Trinitarian app → tap Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Your account will be anonymised immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email us at privacy@trinitarian.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

For pastors: deleting your account will also remove your pastor profile and all uploaded sermons from the platform permanently.

DMCA POLICY

Effective: 1 June 2026

Trinitarian is operated by Globiq Media Technologies Ltd. We respect intellectual property rights and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and equivalent international copyright laws.

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe content on Trinitarian infringes your copyright, send a written notice to:

Email: legal@trinitarian.app

Your notice must include:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorised representative
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  3. Identification of the infringing material on Trinitarian with enough detail to locate it (e.g. URL or sermon title)
  4. Your name, address, telephone number and email address
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  6. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and you are authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Our Response

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, Trinitarian will:

  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content promptly
  • Notify the user who uploaded the content
  • Take action under our repeat infringer policy where applicable

Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed in error, submit a counter-notification to legal@trinitarian.app including:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the removed content and its location before removal
  3. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you have a good faith belief the content was removed by mistake or misidentification
  4. Your name, address and telephone number
  5. A statement consenting to jurisdiction of your local federal court

If a valid counter-notification is received, we may restore the content after 10–14 business days unless the original complainant files a court action.

Repeat Infringers

Trinitarian will terminate accounts of users who are found to be repeat copyright infringers in accordance with our Terms of Service.

Contact

All DMCA-related matters: legal@trinitarian.app

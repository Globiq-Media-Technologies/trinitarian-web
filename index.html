PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

Trinitarian is operated by Globiq Media Technologies Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Full name and display name
  • Email address
  • Username and password (encrypted)
  • Denomination and church information (for pastors)
  • Country and city

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Sermon viewing and listening history
  • IP address and approximate location

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify pastor applications
  • To provide sermon recommendations
  • To send notifications about new sermons and live streams
  • To improve the app and develop new features
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with service providers under strict confidentiality, or when required by law.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using HTTPS/TLS encryption. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact: privacy@trinitarian.app

6. Children's Privacy

Trinitarian is not directed at children under 13.

7. Content Ownership

All sermon content remains the intellectual property of the uploading pastor. Trinitarian holds only a non-exclusive streaming licence. All content is protected under DMCA.

8. Contact Us

  • Email: privacy@trinitarian.app
  • Website: trinitarian.app
  • Company: Globiq Media Technologies Ltd

Pastor Verification

Apply to become a verified pastor on Trinitarian

📋 Verification Requirements

  • Valid ordination certificate from a recognised Christian denomination
  • Ordination body or authority
  • Minimum 2 years of active ministry
  • Church name, country and city
  • Personal statement (minimum 100 words)
  • Verified email address

⚠️ False information will result in permanent account suspension.

📎 After submitting, email your ordination certificate to: verify@trinitarian.app

Please use your registered email address as the subject line.
